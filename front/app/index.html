Future. You are a trainee in the Turing police - a special body overseeing activities in IT and controlling artificial intelligence. Recently, an incident occurred in the organization, after which most of the workers suffered and can not perform their duties. So - it's your time to go into the thick of the events and investigate the crimes!Are you ready to go down to the very bottom of the information marsh and understand the criminal's logic, from the petty crook to the hacker groups? To understand a criminal you need to know and be able to use his methods, so you have to go the same way as an attacker, use the same skills to achieve the result and catch it. You will have to work with the "ice" system - a protective complex from programs and viruses that uses the memory and consciousness of "harvested" hackers who were not too lucky to restore the crime pictures and help rebuild security systems.
